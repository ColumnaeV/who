Studying to Become A Surgeon

This practise of medicine has been a focus of my life for quite some time. Although it may have not been my first choice for occupational study, it has been at the back of my mind from when I was a very young lad. Witnessing tramatic events at a young age combined with my desire to care for others has been slowly feuling my drive into the field of medicine. There are many ways in which a person can help a larger population in humanitarian aid, but by serving in an area where an individual is requiring immediate attention has interested me more. Ultimately, by knowing the individual I helped on a personal level is a satisfaction of life in which I strive to observe every day.

Continuing with Frontend Development

Even though my career interests lie elsewhere, I still find enjoyment in developing in frontend design. I see frontend design as a canvas of art, rather than a method of profit. Every website, graphical interface, and/or app demonstrates the developers creativity and ingenuity in digital art. For me, web-based coding was my limitless canvas that I could express my contemporary designs, ideas, and emotions. The reason why I decided to not pursue this area of expertise as a career is because I knew I would lose this as a hobby. Life should be enjoying what we love, regardless of how marketable it could be.
